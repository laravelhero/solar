Energy is one of the few major operating costs a business can actually *eliminate*. That's why commercial solar consistently delivers returns that rival or beat most other capital projects.

## The four levers of commercial solar ROI

### 1. Direct energy savings
Every kilowatt-hour your array produces is one you don't buy from the utility. For energy-intensive operations — refrigeration, manufacturing, data — this alone is substantial.

### 2. Demand-charge reduction
Commercial bills include **demand charges** based on your highest 15-minute spike of usage. Solar (especially paired with storage) shaves those peaks, attacking a line item most businesses don't even realize they can control.

### 3. The 30% Investment Tax Credit
Businesses claim the same **30% ITC** as homeowners — a dollar-for-dollar reduction in federal tax owed on the full system cost.

### 4. MACRS accelerated depreciation
This is the lever homeowners don't get. Commercial solar can be **depreciated** under MACRS — often with bonus depreciation — letting you write off most of the system's cost in the first few years. Combined with the ITC, the after-tax cost of the system drops dramatically.

## A simplified example

Consider a **$500,000** commercial array:

- **30% ITC:** −$150,000
- **MACRS depreciation benefit:** roughly −$95,000 (varies by tax bracket)
- **Effective net cost:** about **$255,000**
- **Annual energy + demand savings:** ~$55,000

That's a **payback under 5 years** and 20+ years of near-free power afterward — an internal rate of return most CFOs would approve in a heartbeat.

## Financing without touching capital

Don't want to tie up cash? Options include:

- **PPAs** — buy the power, not the system; $0 capital outlay
- **Operating leases** — off-balance-sheet treatment
- **C-PACE financing** — long-term, property-assessed financing in eligible states

## Beyond the spreadsheet

The hard ROI is compelling on its own, but solar also delivers:

- A hedge against **volatile utility rates**
- Measurable progress on **ESG and Scope 2** goals
- A visible **sustainability story** for customers and recruits
